Project Name
Accumly
Project Role
Wordpress Design
Project Year
2024
For the Accumly digital launch project, I was responsible for creating comprehensive digital assets to ensure a strong market presence for this AI-powered FinOps tool. This included the design and development of a WordPress-driven website, focused on user experience and cloud-based financial insights. I crafted a sleek, intuitive interface that aligns with Accumly’s mission to optimize tech spending and financial operations.
Logo

The logo I designed for Accumly reflects the innovative and tech-driven nature of the platform. Through clean, modern lines and a minimalist approach, the logo communicates trust and precision, two key pillars of Accumly’s brand. I chose a geometric form to symbolize trust and growth, which aligns with the core functionality of the FinOps tool. The design is versatile and scalable, ensuring it works across various mediums—from web to print—while maintaining its visual impact.

Color Scheme

The color palette for Accumly was carefully selected to evoke a sense of professionalism, innovation, and reliability. I used a primary palette of Green and neutral Whites, which convey stability and confidence, critical traits in the financial technology space. Accent colors were incorporated to highlight key areas of the site, drawing attention to call-to-action buttons and essential information without overwhelming the viewer. The colors also enhance the overall user experience by creating a visually cohesive and engaging environment.

Typography

For the Accumly brand, I opted for a modern sans-serif font that combines readability with a sleek, professional look. The typography is designed to reinforce the brand’s image as a forward-thinking, technology-driven solution while ensuring readability across various platforms. I carefully selected font pairings that complement each other, striking a balance between body text legibility and bold, attention-grabbing headlines. This approach ensures that the communication is clear, professional, and consistent across all digital assets.
