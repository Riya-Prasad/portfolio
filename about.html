I'm Riya Prasad — I am a passionate UX Designer with a strong foundation in crafting seamless, user-centered digital experiences. My role as a UX Designer involves conducting user research, creating personas, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing to ensure products meet both user needs and business goals. I collaborate closely with developers, product managers, and stakeholders to build intuitive interfaces that provide meaningful user interactions. I also focus on information architecture, interaction design, and accessibility to ensure inclusive, user-friendly solutions. Before transitioning fully into UX design, I spent few years working as a frontend developer, where I honed my skills in turning design ideas into functional, responsive code. During that time, I dabbled in UI/UX tasks, which sparked my interest in understanding user behavior and improving the user experience. To deepen my expertise, I pursued several online degrees in UX, which helped me gain a more comprehensive and professional approach to design. Today, I am fully dedicated to growing as a UX designer, constantly refining my skills to stay updated with industry trends. Outside of work, I enjoy reading books, photography, and binge-watching my favorite shows.

I'm always down to collaborate when I have the time. Reach out and let's chat!
